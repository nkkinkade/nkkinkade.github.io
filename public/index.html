Who I Am

Prior to joining my fellow students at DeltaV I had a wide variety of careers, working from one temporary position to another. A Jack of all trades if you will. But my favorite positions were those where I got to work behind a computer. I was tired of going from one job to the next and wanted something permanent. Something I could grow in. Something that I enjoyed doing. That's where Software Development came in. It is like a puzzle, piece by piece coming together to make a final product. I find that I'm adept at styling pages for that very reason. Ideally I would like to work in an office, in a tech company, preferably in the video game industry. I look forward to the future and where being a developer will take me.
